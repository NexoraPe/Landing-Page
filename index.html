About Us

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need internet to use the app?

The app works offline. All reports are stored locally and automatically synced once online.

Can it integrate with our existing systems?

Yes, the Corporate Plan includes API access and custom integrations.

Does it comply with safety regulations?

SafeWork follows ISO 45001 and can adapt to your country’s laws.

Is training required to use SafeWork?

No, it's intuitive. Optional training is available for admins.

How secure is the data stored?

We use encryption, cloud infrastructure, and regular backups.

What happens if we need help or support?

All plans include support. Premium users get priority or dedicated agents.

Can users submit anonymous reports?

Yes. Anonymous reporting can be enabled or disabled by the admin.

Is there a mobile version?

Yes. SafeWork works on web, Android and iOS devices, fully responsive.
